Title: Spaghetti Carbonara
Servings: 4
Tags: dinner, italian, pasta
Ingredients:
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 6 large eggs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 8 oz pancetta, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup white wine
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tsp black pepper
- Salt to taste
Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente
2. Meanwhile, cook pancetta in a large skillet until crispy
3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
4. Add white wine and let it reduce by half
5. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper
6. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water
7. Add hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta
8. Remove from heat and quickly toss with egg mixture
9. Add pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce
10. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately
