Title: Italian Wedding Soup
Servings: 12
Tags: soup, italian, comfort food
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 lb Italian sausage
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp olive oil, divided
- 1/2 large white onion, chopped
- 3 large carrots, chopped
- 3 celery ribs, chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1 tsp dried parsley
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup acini de pepe pasta
- 4 oz baby spinach
Instructions:
1. Mix ground beef, Italian sausage, breadcrumbs, parmesan, egg, parsley, garlic powder, and salt in a bowl.
2. Roll mixture into small meatballs (about 2 tsp each).
3. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat and brown meatballs for 5 to 6 minutes, turning often.
4. Remove meatballs and set aside.
5. Heat remaining 2 tbsp olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
6. Add onion, carrots, and celery and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
7. Add garlic, oregano, basil, dried parsley, and black pepper and cook for 2 minutes.
8. Add chicken broth and water and bring to a boil.
9. Add meatballs and pasta, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.
10. Stir in spinach until wilted and serve warm.
