Title: The Best Meatloaf Recipe
Servings: 4
Tags: dinner, main course, comfort food

Ingredients:
- 2 lb ground beef, 80–85% lean
- 1 med onion, finely chopped
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 2 large eggs
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp ketchup
- 3 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 3/4 cup Panko breadcrumbs
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 3/4 cup ketchup (for glaze)
- 1 1/2 tsp white vinegar
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder (for glaze)
- 1/2 tsp onion powder (for glaze)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment or foil.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Saute chopped onion until softened and golden, about 5–7 minutes; cool slightly.
4. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, sautéed onion, eggs, minced garlic, 2 tbsp ketchup, parsley, breadcrumbs, milk, salt, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
5. Mix just until combined and shape mixture into a loaf on the prepared baking sheet.
6. Bake uncovered at 350°F for 40 minutes.
7. Meanwhile, in a small bowl mix 3/4 cup ketchup, white vinegar, brown sugar, garlic powder, and onion powder to make the glaze.
8. Spread glaze over the partially baked meatloaf.
9. Return meatloaf to oven and bake an additional 20 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 160°F.
10. Let rest 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
