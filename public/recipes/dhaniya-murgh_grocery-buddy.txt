Title: Quick Dhaniya Murgh (Coriander Chicken Curry)
Servings: 2
Tags: curry, indian, chicken
Ingredients:
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 inch ginger, minced
- 250 g Greek yogurt
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 15 g fresh coriander, chopped
- 2 tbsp oil
- salt, to taste
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add onion and cook for 4 to 5 minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 1 to 2 minutes until fragrant.
4. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt and stir for 30 seconds.
5. Add chicken thighs and brown for 2 to 3 minutes per side.
6. Reduce heat to low and stir in yogurt gradually, stirring constantly.
7. Cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
8. Stir in garam masala and most of the fresh coriander.
9. Simmer for 2 minutes, garnish with remaining coriander, and serve.
