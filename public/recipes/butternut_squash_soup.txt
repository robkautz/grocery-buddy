Title: Butternut Squash Soup
Servings: 4
Tags: soup, vegetarian, fall

Ingredients:
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- 1 medium carrot, chopped
- 2 medium potatoes, cubed
- 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cubed
- 4 cups chicken stock
- salt to taste
- black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion, celery, carrot, potatoes, and butternut squash.
3. Cook vegetables until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
4. Pour in chicken stock until vegetables are covered.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
6. Cover and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 40 minutes.
7. Carefully blend soup until smooth.
8. Return soup to the pot and season with salt and black pepper.
9. Serve hot.
