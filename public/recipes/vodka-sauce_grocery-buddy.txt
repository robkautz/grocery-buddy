Title: Vodka Sauce
Servings: 4
Tags: pasta, italian, sauce
Ingredients:
- 1 lb pasta (penne or rigatoni)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup vodka
- 28 oz crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- salt, to taste
- black pepper, to taste
- fresh basil, chopped (optional)
Instructions:
1. Cook pasta in salted water until al dente and reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Add onion and cook for 4 to 5 minutes until softened.
4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 1 minute.
5. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
6. Add vodka and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes.
8. Reduce heat to low and stir in heavy cream until smooth.
9. Stir in parmesan and season with salt and black pepper.
10. Add pasta and toss to coat, adding pasta water as needed.
11. Serve with basil and extra parmesan if desired.
