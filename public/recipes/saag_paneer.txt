Title: Saag Paneer
Servings: 2
Tags: indian, vegetarian, spinach, dinner

Ingredients:
- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 onions, chopped
- 1 lb spinach
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 8 oz paneer, cubed
- salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Brown 2 onions in 1 tbsp oil.
2. Blanch 1 lb spinach and put to the side.
3. Add 3 cloves garlic (minced) and 1 inch grated ginger.
4. Add 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp coriander, and 1/4 tsp cayenne.
5. Add 2 chopped tomatoes and simmer.
6. Chop or blend spinach and add to mixture.
7. Brown paneer and add to mixture.
8. Add salt to taste.
